## Cau hoi (<= 200 chu)

Tren golden set, BM25 manh nhat o query `exact` vi query trung truc tiep tu khoa trong corpus. Vector search phu hop hon khi user dien giai/paraphrase, vi no bat y nghia gan dung thay vi chi khop chu. Voi query `mixed`, hybrid RRF tot nhat vi ket hop tin hieu lexical cua BM25 va tin hieu semantic cua vector search, nen on dinh hon tren nhieu kieu query. Toi se khong dung hybrid khi query rat ngan va exact, khi latency/cost la uu tien cuc cao, hoac khi mot mode don le da du tot va he thong can don gian de van hanh.
